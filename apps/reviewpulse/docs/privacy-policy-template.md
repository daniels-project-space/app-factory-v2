# Privacy Policy for ReviewPulse

**Last updated:** April 11, 2026

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect your email address and any profile information you provide, including your business name.

### Customer Contact Data
To send SMS review requests, you provide customer names and phone numbers. This information is stored securely and used solely for sending review request messages on your behalf.

### SMS Messaging Data
We record the review requests you send, including recipient phone numbers, message content, delivery status, and timestamps. Customers who reply STOP are immediately opted out and will not receive further messages from your account.

### Usage Data
We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app experience. This includes screens viewed, features used, and app performance data.

### Device Information
We collect device type, operating system version, and a unique device identifier for push notifications and crash reporting.

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the app service
- To send SMS review requests to your customers via Twilio
- To send push notifications (with your permission)
- To improve the app based on usage patterns
- To provide customer support
- To detect and prevent technical issues

## SMS Messaging

ReviewPulse uses Twilio to deliver SMS review request messages to your customers. When you send a review request:

- The customer's phone number and name are transmitted to Twilio for message delivery
- Customers can reply STOP at any time to opt out; opt-outs are honored immediately and permanently
- Standard message and data rates may apply for your customers
- You are responsible for obtaining appropriate consent from customers before sending SMS messages

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS). All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Customer phone numbers are stored only as long as your account is active or until you delete them.

## Third-Party Services

- **Supabase**: Database and authentication
- **Twilio**: SMS message delivery — customer phone numbers are shared with Twilio solely for message delivery
- **Sentry**: Crash reporting and error tracking
- **PostHog**: Anonymous usage analytics
- **RevenueCat**: Subscription management and billing

## Your Rights

You can request to:
- Access your personal data
- Delete your account and all associated data (including all customer contact data)
- Export your data
- Opt out of analytics
