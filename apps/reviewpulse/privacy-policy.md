# ReviewPulse Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: March 12, 2026
**Last Updated**: March 12, 2026

ReviewPulse ("we", "our", "us") operates the ReviewPulse mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect your email address, business name, and business type. This information is required to provide our services.

### Business Profile Data
When you connect a Google Business Profile or Yelp account, we access your business reviews, ratings, and profile information through official APIs. We store this data to display your review feed and analytics.

### Customer Contact Information
When you use the SMS review request feature, you provide customer names and phone numbers. We store this information to send review requests and track delivery status.

### SMS Data
We use Twilio to send SMS messages on your behalf. Message content, delivery status, and opt-out responses are stored to maintain compliance and provide delivery tracking.

### Usage Data
We collect anonymous usage analytics (screen views, feature usage, session duration) through PostHog to improve the App. No personally identifiable information is included in analytics data.

### Device Information
We collect push notification tokens to deliver review alerts. We do not collect device identifiers, location data, or other device information beyond what is necessary for push notifications.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- **Provide Services**: Display reviews, send SMS requests, generate AI reply suggestions, deliver push notifications
- **AI Reply Generation**: When you request an AI-drafted reply, the review text and your business information are sent to our AI provider (Anthropic Claude) to generate a professional response. We do not train AI models on your data.
- **Analytics**: Aggregate anonymized usage data to improve App features and performance
- **Communications**: Send transactional emails (password reset, account notifications)

## 3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with:

- **Twilio**: SMS delivery (customer phone numbers and message content)
- **Google/Yelp**: Review data access and reply posting through official APIs
- **Anthropic**: Review text for AI reply generation (processed, not stored)
- **RevenueCat**: Subscription management (purchase status only)
- **Supabase**: Secure cloud database hosting (all data encrypted at rest)

## 4. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). OAuth tokens for Google Business Profile are encrypted with AES-256-GCM before storage. We follow industry-standard security practices.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account data**: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
- **Review data**: Synced from platforms while connected. Deleted when you disconnect a platform or delete your account.
- **Customer contacts**: Retained while your account is active. Deleted with account deletion.
- **SMS records**: Retained for 12 months for compliance, then deleted.

## 6. Your Rights

You can:
- **Access** your data through the App's settings screen
- **Delete** your account and all associated data through the App
- **Disconnect** Google or Yelp profiles at any time
- **Opt out** of analytics by contacting support
- **Export** your review data by contacting support

For GDPR/CCPA requests, contact privacy@reviewpulse.app.

## 7. SMS Compliance (TCPA)

- SMS messages are sent only to numbers you provide with your express consent
- Every SMS includes opt-out instructions ("Reply STOP to unsubscribe")
- Opted-out numbers are immediately blocked from future messages
- We maintain consent records with timestamps

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated through the App. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

## 10. Contact

For privacy questions: privacy@reviewpulse.app
